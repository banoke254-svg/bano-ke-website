BANO KE

A futuristic mobile marble battle arena.

Built for mobile. Designed to compete.

Problem

Mobile players often choose between simple games that quickly become repetitive and competitive games that require too much time to learn.

  • Players want games that are easy to understand.
  • They want short matches for mobile sessions.
  • They want competition, social play, and progression.
  • They want a game that feels exciting and visually unique.

Solution

BANO KE combines the accessibility of traditional marble games with the excitement of a modern multiplayer battle arena.

Players aim, control power, outsmart opponents, eliminate rivals, and enter the hole to win. Every match rewards precision, timing, and smart decision-making.

Game Overview

  • Aim and control the power of every shot.
  • Hit and eliminate opposing marbles.
  • Use positioning and arena strategy.
  • Enter the hole to score and win.
  • Earn coins and gold.
  • Unlock premium marbles and cosmetics.
  • Compete in online rooms and rankings.

Target Audience

  • Mobile gamers who enjoy short competitive matches.
  • Casual players looking for deeper gameplay.
  • Competitive players who enjoy rankings and mastery.
  • African gamers seeking locally inspired experiences.
  • Friends and online communities looking for social competition.
  • Content creators interested in tournaments and challenges.

Market Opportunity

Mobile gaming is one of the biggest entertainment markets in the world. BANO KE is positioned for quick mobile play, online competition, cosmetics, cultural identity, and creator-led growth.

  • Free-to-play mobile games are easy to share.
  • Smartphone adoption is growing across Africa.
  • Players enjoy short competitive multiplayer experiences.
  • Cosmetics and personalization create strong revenue opportunities.

Business Model

BANO KE will be free to download, reducing the barrier for new players.

  • Premium marble skins.
  • Trails, banners, nameplates, and arena themes.
  • Gold and in-game currency purchases.
  • Seasonal battle passes.
  • Optional rewarded advertisements.
  • Sponsored tournaments and live events.
  • Creator and African brand partnerships.

Growth Strategy

  • Players discover the game through creators, clips, and referrals.
  • Quick onboarding converts interest into gameplay.
  • Rankings and rivalries encourage players to return.
  • Custom marbles and wins create shareable moments.
  • Tournaments and seasonal events bring players back.
  • Community engagement creates organic growth.

Progress So Far

  • Core 3D marble gameplay.
  • Aiming, shooting, scoring, and elimination systems.
  • AI-controlled opponents.
  • Online multiplayer rooms and relay server.
  • Tutorial and rules experience.
  • Showroom and marble customization.
  • Coins, gold, unlock systems, settings, and UI design.
  • Android APK export development.

Roadmap

  • Add daily rewards and player challenges.
  • Introduce seasons and battle passes.
  • Expand marbles, trails, arenas, and cultural themes.
  • Run closed and open beta tests.
  • Optimize performance across Android devices.
  • Build creator and gaming-community partnerships.
  • Launch the polished Android version.
