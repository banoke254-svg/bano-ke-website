Problem

Mobile players often have to choose between simple games that quickly become repetitive and competitive games that require too much time to learn.

Players need a game that is:

  • Easy to understand and start playing
  • Competitive enough to reward skill
  • Short enough for quick mobile sessions
  • Social, replayable, and visually exciting
  • Built around meaningful progression
A futuristic mobile marble battle arena.

Solution

BANO combines the accessibility of traditional marble games with the excitement of a modern multiplayer battle arena.

Players aim, control their power, outsmart opponents, eliminate rivals, and enter the hole to win. Every match rewards precision, strategy, and intelligent decision-making.

A futuristic mobile marble battle arena.

Game Overview

BANO transforms familiar marble gameplay into a competitive digital sport.

Players can:

  • Aim and control the power of every shot
  • Hit and eliminate opposing marbles
  • Use positioning and arena strategy
  • Enter the hole to score and win
  • Earn coins and gold
  • Unlock premium marbles and cosmetic items
  • Compete in online rooms
  • Climb the ranking system

Business Model

BANO will be free to download, reducing the barrier for new players.

Revenue opportunities include:

  • Premium marble skins
  • Trails, banners, nameplates, and arena themes
  • Gold and in-game currency purchases
  • Seasonal battle passes
  • Limited-edition cosmetic collections
  • Optional rewarded advertisements
  • Sponsored tournaments and live events
  • Future partnerships with creators and African brands

The model focuses on selling identity and progression without making the game unfair or pay-to-win.
